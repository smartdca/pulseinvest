    Smart DCA · 101

    Four steps to
    invest like a pro.

    No finance degree needed. Just four ideas that separate investors who build wealth from those who don't.

    Not investment advice · Past ≠ Future
    Step 01
    First, check the history.
    ❌  "My friend said this stock is going to moon."
    ✅  Run a backtest. Compare it against the market. Let the numbers speak.
    Before committing to any stock or crypto, use the Backtest tool to see how it performed over its full history — and how Smart DCA would have done vs. just buying the index.
    → Try Backtest
    Step 02
    Never stop.
    Especially when it drops.
    ❌  "The market is crashing. I'll pause and wait for it to recover."
    ✅  The months you skip are the months you miss the cheapest prices.
    Bear markets, crashes, bubbles — every instinct says stop. Don't. History shows that the people who kept investing through downturns ended up far ahead. A good rule of thumb: invest 10–20% of monthly income, at a level you can sustain for years, not just months.
    Step 03
    Know when to
    go bigger.
    ❌  "I invest $500 every month, same amount, no matter what."
    ✅  When RSI hits a historic low, add more. When markets are stretched, maintain. Let the formula decide.
    Regular DCA invests the same amount every time. Smart DCA adjusts — more when the signal is strong, normal when it's not. Every time you're about to invest, check the calculator first.
    → Try Calculator
    Step 04
    Multiple stocks?
    Rank them.
    ❌  "I have 5 stocks. I just split my budget evenly every month."
    ✅  Add them all to your Watchlist. Let the DCA Score tell you which one deserves more this month.
    You don't have time to track every stock every day. Your Watchlist does it for you — sorting all your holdings by opportunity score, so the best entry point is always at the top.
